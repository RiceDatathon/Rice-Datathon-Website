What's a Datathon?

A datathon is a multi-day event for data science enthusiasts to come together, learn, and compete in exciting data science challenges! There will be prizes for winning teams, opportunities to network, and lots of good food. Register for Rice Datathon 2025 today!

FAQ
When and where is the Datathon?

Rice Datathon 2025 will take place January 31st-February 2nd, 2025, on Rice University's campus.

Who can attend?

Undergraduate or graduate students are encouraged to attend this physical-first event, regardless of where they're from. Students of all majors and schools of study are welcome.

When do registrations open?

Official Registration is open now! Fill out the registration form→ to reserve your spot and join our mailing list→!

How much will it cost?

There is no cost for attending Rice Datathon! We'll provide the resources () for you to participate comfortably.

Is there an option to participate virtually?

Rice Datathon 2025 is a physical-first event and it is expected for attendees to participate in-person. Virtual accommodations will only be available for select events and sessions.

Have more questions?

Feel free to reach out to datathon@rice.edu with any additional questions you have!
